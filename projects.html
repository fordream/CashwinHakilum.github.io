Supermassive Journey

This team project is an 8-bit turn-based strategy game developed entirely with JAVA. The objective of this game is to command your fleet of starships to victory by destroying the enemy fleet's mothership while supporting and defending your own. I coded the entire gameplay component of the in-house engine, along with three friends who directed the project, developed the graphics to be used in the GUI, and aided me in coding the AI respectively. This game will be released publicly when it's done, as we have ceased further development of this project since its inception and development throughout Winter 2013. The project may continue soon, though, so stay tuned!
